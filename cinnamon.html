Cinnamon (Kayu Manis)

Indonesian cinnamon, or "Kayu Manis", is renowned for its rich aroma and strong flavor, making it a top choice in the global spice market. Beukeuh Eksporind sources cinnamon directly from sustainable farms, ensuring purity and high essential oil content. Our cinnamon is carefully dried and graded to meet international quality standards for culinary, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries.
