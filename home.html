Welcome to Beukeuh Eksporind

Beukeuh Eksporind is your trusted partner in sourcing and exporting high-quality natural commodities from Indonesia. We specialize in bentonite, vanilla, cinnamon, and coffee — products that represent the richness of Indonesia's natural resources and cultural heritage.

With a commitment to sustainability and excellence, we ensure our products meet international standards. Our mission is to connect local farmers and producers to the global market with integrity and professionalism.
