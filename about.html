About Beukeuh Eksporind

Connecting Indonesia's nature to the world.

Who We Are

Beukeuh Eksporind is an Indonesian export company specializing in high-quality natural commodities such as Bentonite, vanilla, cinnamon, charcoal, and more.

With strong principles of integrity and sustainability, we aim to connect Indonesia’s natural resources to global markets through long-term partnerships and professional service.

Our Vision

To become a leading exporter and reliable partner that represents Indonesia’s natural wealth with trust, transparency, and excellence.
