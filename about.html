About Beukeuh Eksporind

Beukeuh Eksporind is a trusted Indonesian export company that specializes in delivering the richness of nature through premium-quality commodities. From Bentonite to vanilla, cinnamon, coffee, and charcoal, we take pride in our local resources and sustainable practices.

Our mission is to connect Indonesian farmers and producers with the global market through fair trade, transparency, and long-term partnerships. We ensure consistent quality, ethical sourcing, and customer-focused service.

With a growing presence and reputation in the export industry, Beukeuh Eksporind aims to be your reliable partner in sourcing the best of Indonesia.
