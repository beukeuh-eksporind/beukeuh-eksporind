About Us

Beukeuh Eksporind is an Indonesian company dedicated to exporting high-quality natural commodities. With a strong focus on sustainability and quality, we bring Indonesia’s rich resources — such as bentonite, vanilla, cinnamon, and coffee — to the world. Our team works closely with local producers to ensure fair trade, environmental responsibility, and international standards.
