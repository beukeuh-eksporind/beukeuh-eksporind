About Beukeuh Eksporind

Connecting Indonesia's nature to the world.

Who We Are

Beukeuh Eksporind is an Indonesian export company that focuses on the global distribution of high-quality natural products such as Bentonite, vanilla, cinnamon, charcoal, and more. Our mission is to represent the richness of Indonesia's natural resources with integrity and sustainable practices.

We aim to create long-term relationships with our partners and clients by offering reliable service, consistent quality, and transparent business processes.
