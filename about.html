About Us

Beukeuh Eksporind is a trusted export company based in Indonesia, committed to delivering high-quality natural commodities such as bentonite, vanilla, cinnamon, and coffee to global markets.

We collaborate with local farmers and producers, ensuring sustainability, traceability, and consistency in every shipment. Our mission is to bring the richness of Indonesian nature to the world.

Our team is passionate about excellence, transparency, and building long-term international partnerships.
