About Us

Beukeuh Eksporind is a company committed to exporting premium natural commodities from Indonesia — including Bentonite, charcoal, spices, and other agricultural products.

We prioritize sustainability, local empowerment, and long-term partnerships rooted in trust and mutual growth. With deep ties to local producers and a global outlook, our mission is to bring the best of Indonesia’s nature to the world.
