About Us

Beukeuh Eksporind is a trusted Indonesian exporter of high-quality natural commodities. We specialize in supplying bentonite, vanilla, cinnamon, and coffee — all sourced sustainably from across Indonesia.

Our mission is to bring the richness of Indonesia's natural resources to the global market. With a focus on quality, professionalism, and reliability, we support international clients in fulfilling their commodity needs efficiently and transparently.

Backed by experienced professionals and a growing network of suppliers, Beukeuh Eksporind is ready to deliver consistent value and build long-term export partnerships.
