About Us

Beukeuh Eksporind is an Indonesian company engaged in the export of natural commodities such as bentonite, vanilla, cinnamon, and coffee. We are committed to maintaining quality, sustainability, and integrity in every aspect of our business.

With experience and a wide network, we serve as a bridge between local farmers and global markets. Our goal is to introduce Indonesia's natural wealth to the world through fairness, transparency, and sustainable practices.
