About Us

Beukeuh Eksporind is an Indonesian company dedicated to delivering the best of our natural resources to the world. With a strong commitment to quality, sustainability, and global standards, we focus on exporting key Indonesian commodities including bentonite, vanilla, cinnamon, and coffee. Our team ensures every product meets international requirements while maintaining authenticity and local value.

We believe in long-term partnerships and transparent trade practices. Whether you're a global buyer or regional distributor, Beukeuh Eksporind is here to support your business needs with integrity and professionalism.
