Export-Grade Indonesian Coffee

Indonesia is known for its rich and aromatic coffee. We export robusta and arabica beans sourced from Sumatra, Java, and Sulawesi. Available in green bean, roasted, or powder form.

Our coffee is grown by sustainable farmers, sorted by hand, and processed under international quality standards.

  • Types: Arabica, Robusta
  • Form: Green bean, roasted, or powder
  • Moisture: < 13%
  • Packaging: Jute bag, 60 kg or custom
  • Origin: Sumatra, Java, Sulawesi
