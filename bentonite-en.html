Bentonite

Versatile Clay Mineral for Global Industries

Why Choose Our Bentonite?

  • Excellent absorbent and binding capacity
  • Free from harmful contaminants
  • Available in powder or granular form
  • Suitable for foundry, drilling fluid, fertilizer, cosmetics, and more
