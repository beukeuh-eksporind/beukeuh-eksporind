Product Description

Bentonite is a naturally occurring clay mineral widely used in drilling, purification, agriculture, and food processing industries. Our product is sourced from high-quality mines in Indonesia, processed under export standards, and packed to suit international market needs.

General Specifications:

  • Color: Light gray to beige
  • Moisture content: < 12%
  • Type: Sodium and calcium bentonite
  • Applications: Drilling, agriculture, cosmetics, animal feed

Packaging:

Available in 25kg and 50kg bags, or customized bulk packaging.
