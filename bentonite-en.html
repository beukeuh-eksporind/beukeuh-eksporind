What is Bentonite?

Bentonite is a natural clay formed from volcanic ash, known for its excellent absorption and binding properties. At Beukeuh Eksporind, we supply high-quality bentonite suitable for foundries, drilling fluids, wastewater treatment, agriculture, and more.

Applications

Industrial Use

Used as binder for foundry molds and as lubricant in drilling operations.

Agriculture

Improves soil quality, acts as carrier for fertilizers and pesticides.

Water Treatment

Effective in absorbing impurities and heavy metals from wastewater.
