About Us

Committed to Exporting Natural Minerals from Indonesia

Beukeuh Eksporind is an Indonesian export company specialized in supplying high-quality natural minerals and agricultural commodities. With a focus on bentonite, zeolite, coconut charcoal, and premium spices, we ensure every product meets international standards.

We are proud to be a trusted partner for global industries — from agriculture and energy to manufacturing and food & beverage sectors.

With a commitment to sustainability and quality, Beukeuh Eksporind connects Indonesia’s natural richness to the global market.
