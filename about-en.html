Company Profile

Beukeuh Eksporind is an Indonesian export company focusing on natural commodities such as bentonite, zeolite, and vanilla. We are committed to delivering high-quality products that meet international export standards while empowering local farmers and producers.

We believe Indonesia’s strength lies in its natural wealth and sincere collaboration. Through a transparent and professional approach, Beukeuh Eksporind builds a bridge between local resources and global markets.

Vision & Mission

  • Vision: To become a trusted partner for Indonesian export commodities in global markets.
  • Mission:
    • Consistently provide high-quality export products.
    • Support the welfare of local farming and production partners.
    • Build long-term, mutually beneficial relationships.
