About Us

Natural Mineral Exporter from Indonesia to the World

Beukeuh Eksporind is a company focused on exporting high-quality natural minerals such as bentonite, zeolite, and agricultural commodities from Indonesia to the global market. We are committed to maintaining product quality, timely delivery, and sustainability.

Through a wide network of farmers, miners, and logistics partners, we provide materials that meet various industry specifications, available in bulk or export-ready packaging.

We believe that Indonesia’s natural resources hold solutions for numerous global industries — from organic farming and water treatment to modern manufacturing.
