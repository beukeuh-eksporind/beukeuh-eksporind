Zeolite

Versatile Mineral for Water Purification and Agriculture

Zeolite is a natural mineral known for its excellent absorption and filtration capabilities. Our zeolite products are ideal for use in water purification systems, organic farming, and animal feed supplements. Processed hygienically and sourced from Indonesia's finest reserves, our zeolite meets international export standards.

Why Choose Our Zeolite?

  • High absorption for water and odor filtration
  • Great for organic soil and fertilizer blends
  • Supports animal health in feed formulations
  • Consistent quality and free from contaminants
  • Available in various granule sizes
