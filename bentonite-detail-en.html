Product Detail: Bentonite

Versatile Natural Clay Mineral from Indonesia

Description

Bentonite is a naturally occurring clay mineral with excellent absorbent and swelling properties. It is widely used in foundry, oil drilling, water treatment, and agriculture sectors. Our bentonite is sourced from the finest Indonesian mines and processed to meet international quality standards.

Main Applications

  • 👉 Metal casting (foundry)
  • 👉 Oil & gas drilling support
  • 👉 Wastewater treatment systems
  • 👉 Soil conditioning & livestock feed

Form & Packaging

  • 🔹 Available Forms: Powder & Granules
  • 🔹 Packaging: 25kg / 50kg polypropylene bags, or 1-ton jumbo bags
  • 🔹 Private label available upon request

General Technical Specs

  • ✅ Moisture Content: Max 12%
  • ✅ Swelling Capacity: 25–30 ml/2g
  • ✅ Montmorillonite Content: ≥75%
  • ✅ pH Level: 8–10
