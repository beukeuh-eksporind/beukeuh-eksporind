What is Zeolite?

Zeolite is a natural aluminosilicate mineral with exceptional properties in absorption, filtration, and ion exchange. Beukeuh Eksporind provides high-quality Indonesian zeolite for environmental, agricultural, and industrial applications.

Applications of Zeolite

Farming & Aquaculture

Improves water quality by absorbing ammonia and supporting healthier livestock and fish environments.

Agriculture

Acts as a soil conditioner and nutrient binder in organic fertilizers and composting.

Environment & Industry

Filters wastewater, toxic gases, and heavy metals in various industrial processes.
