About Clove

Clove (Syzygium aromaticum) is one of the world’s oldest and most valuable spices. Native to the Indonesian archipelago, clove is widely used in food, beverages, pharmaceuticals, and personal care industries for its strong aroma and rich essential oil content.

We supply high-quality sun-dried clove from trusted local farmers in Indonesia. Our cloves are carefully selected to ensure maximum oil retention and strong aromatic profile.

General Specifications:

  • Origin: Indonesia (Sumatra, Sulawesi)
  • Essential oil content: ≥ 13%
  • Moisture content: ≤ 12%
  • Color: Deep reddish-brown
  • Form: Whole, unbroken
