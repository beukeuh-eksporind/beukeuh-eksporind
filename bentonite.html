Bentonite

Bentonite is a natural clay with exceptional absorbent and swelling properties, making it highly valuable for various industries such as agriculture, drilling, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.

At Beukeuh Eksporind, we supply premium-quality Bentonite from Indonesia, processed to meet global standards. We ensure reliability, consistency, and support for long-term partnerships.
