Bentonite

Bentonite is a naturally occurring clay mineral known for its absorbent and binding properties. It is widely used in industries such as agriculture, construction, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.

Our Bentonite is sourced responsibly from Indonesia and processed to meet international standards. We ensure consistent quality and reliable supply for all your industrial needs.
